Case Study: Local SEO for Real Estate

How I Helped a Business Rank in Google's Top 3
Home > Portfolio > Case Study

📌 Client Overview

A real estate agency in Aligarh aimed to improve online visibility and local lead generation for searches like "real estate agent near me" and "property dealer in Aligarh".

🧑‍💻 My Role

I designed and implemented a comprehensive SEO strategy covering technical SEO, content creation, local citations, and GMB optimization.

🛠️ Actions Taken

  • ✅ Technical SEO Audit with Screaming Frog
  • ✅ Created location-based landing pages with geo-targeted keywords
  • ✅ Optimized Google My Business (GMB) profile
  • ✅ Built 30+ citations across local directories
  • ✅ Implemented LocalBusiness schema markup

📈 Results

  • 🚀 Ranked in Google Local 3-Pack for 6 key phrases
  • 📞 180% increase in GMB-driven inquiries
  • 📊 Improved CTR & engagement on service pages

🧰 Tools Used

SEMrush, Screaming Frog, Google Search Console, BrightLocal, GMB, Canva
